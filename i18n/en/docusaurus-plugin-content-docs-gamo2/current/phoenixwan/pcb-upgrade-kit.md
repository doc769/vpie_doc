# PHOENIXWAN PCB upgrade kit replacement method

<Steps>
<Step title="Prepare tools">

①Phillips screwdriver；②2.5mm Hexagon screwdriver (wrench).

![Step 01](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/01-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Pry off the four corner pads">

Pry off the four corner pads.

![Step 02a](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/14-2.png)

![Step 02b](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/15-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws securing the housing">

Remove the screws securing the housing.

![Step 03](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/33.01.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Separate the frame">

Separate the frame.

![Step 04](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/01-3.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the clips securing the cables">

Remove the clips securing the cables.

![Step 05](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/02-3.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the key panel">

Remove the key panel.

![Step 06](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/03-3.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Unplug all the plugs on the motherboard">

Unplug all the plugs on the motherboard.

![Step 07](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/04-3.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws securing the motherboard">

Remove the screws securing the motherboard.

![Step 08](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/05-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Prepare a new motherboard">

Prepare a new motherboard, ① power cable, ② new motherboard.

![Step 09](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/06-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Fasten the new motherboard to the frame">

Fasten the new motherboard to the frame.

![Step 10](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/07-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Fix the motherboard with screws">

Fix the motherboard with screws.

![Step 11](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/08-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Connect the wiring to the motherboard">

Connect the wiring to the corresponding position of the main board according to the following figure.

![Step 12](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/新主板-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws securing the audio light">

Remove the screws securing the audio light.

![Step 13](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/09-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Replace the audio light">

Remove the old audio light and replace it with a new audio light and fix it.

![Step 14](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/10-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Connect motherboard and audio light">

Please use the "4" wire harness (3pin) to connect the motherboard and the audio light.

![Step 15](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/11-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the gear plate">

Remove the gear plate.

![Step 16](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/12-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Unplug the ring light">

Unplug the ring light.

![Step 17](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/13-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the turntable sticker">

Remove the turntable sticker.

![Step 18](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/17-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws securing the turntable">

Remove the screws securing the turntable.

![Step 19](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/18-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Lift the turntable out">

Press down on one side of the turntable with one hand to lift the other side, while the other hand lifts the turntable out of the raised part.

![Step 20](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/19-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Take out the felt and washer">

Take out the felt and washer.

![Step 21](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/20-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screw fixing the aluminum plate">

Use a 2.5mm hexagon wrench to remove the screw fixing the aluminum plate. Please pay attention to the nut with the fixing screw inside. When removing, please hold down the nut to prevent loss.

![Step 22](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/21-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Use a hex wrench">

Use a hex wrench.

![Step 23](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/22-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Hold down the fixed nut">

Hold down the fixed nut.

![Step 24](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/23-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the turntable part in its entirety">

The turntable part can be removed in its entirety after removing the screws and nuts.

![Step 25](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/24-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the turntable part">

Remove the turntable part.

![Step 26](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/29-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws and remove the silver plate">

Remove the screws and remove the silver plate.

![Step 27](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/14-4.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws and remove the old ring light">

Remove the screws and remove the old ring light.

![Step 28](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/15-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put on a new ring light and fix it">

Put on a new ring light and fix it.

![Step 29](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/16-3.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Reinstall and secure the silver disc">

Reinstall and secure the silver disc.

![Step 30](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/3426B689B66C0A6818543199481F9208.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Connect the ring light harness">

Connect the ring light harness (wire 3) to the ring light.

![Step 31](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/18-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Press the 3-wire in the lower position of the clip">

Note that the 3-wire should be pressed in the lower position of the clip to avoid the gear touching.

![Step 32](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/19-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Reinstall and secure the gear plate">

Reinstall and secure the gear plate.

![Step 33](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/20-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the light guide ring and aluminum plate back in place">

Put the light guide ring and aluminum plate back in place.

![Step 34](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/25-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Tighten the screws and nuts that fix the aluminum plate">

Tighten the screws and nuts that fix the aluminum plate. (After inserting the nut into the fixed position, hold down the nut and tighten the screw.)

![Step 35](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/23-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the felt and washer back in place">

Put the felt and washer back in place, being careful not to overlap.

![Step 36](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/31.01.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the turntable back in place and tighten the screws">

Put the turntable back in place and tighten the screws.

![Step 37](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/31.02-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Fit the upper and lower shells together">

First remove the button panel, align the ① and confirm that the wire at ② will not be stuck, and then fit the upper and lower shells together.

![Step 38](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/32-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Replace the retaining screws and attach the pads">

Replace the retaining screws on the back and attach the pads to the four corners.

![Step 39](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/33.01.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Prepare the Power Adapter Box">

Prepare the Power Adapter Box.

![Step 40](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/21-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Install the adapter">

After tearing off the double-sided tape, install the adapter in a suitable position according to the length of the wiring.

![Step 41](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/22-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Connect the power plug to the adapter">

Connect the power plug to the adapter.

![Step 42](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/23-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Mainboard wiring status after all replacements">

After all the replacements are completed, the mainboard wiring status.

![Step 43](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/pcb-upgrade-kit/24-2.png)

</Step>
</Steps>
