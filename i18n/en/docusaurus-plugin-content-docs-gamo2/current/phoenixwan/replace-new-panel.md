# PHOENIXWAN Replace with new panel 20220420

<Steps>
<Step title="Prepare tools">

①Phillips screwdriver；②2.5mm Hexagon screwdriver (wrench).

![Step 01](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/01-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Panel film is double-sided">

The panel film is double-sided, please note that both sides need to be torn.

![Step 02](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/02-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Replace the button part panel">

Replace the button part panel, press the position of the hexagonal ejector column on the back of the controller.

![Step 03](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/03-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Take out the ejected button panel">

Take out the ejected button panel.

![Step 04](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/04-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the micro switch and LED part">

Rotate counterclockwise to remove the micro switch and LED part. (Please pinch the micro switch for the sanwa button, and pull it out while shaking it left and right.)

![Step 05](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/05-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="After removing all key microswitches">

After removing all key microswitches.

![Step 06](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/06-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the white nut and button">

Remove the white nut and remove the button.

![Step 07](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/07-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="After removing all buttons">

After removing all buttons.

![Step 08](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/08-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Arrange new panels neatly in order">

Arrange new panels neatly in order. (If you like black background, please remove ③ silver cardboard.)

![Step 09](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/09-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the 4 corner buttons first">

Put the 4 corner buttons first to fix the panel position.

![Step 10](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/10-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Secure the four buttons with white nuts">

Use the white nuts to secure the four buttons and install all the buttons back in place.

![Step 11](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/11-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the micro switch back in its original position">

Put the micro switch back in its original position. (The ordinary button is placed in the button according to the angle of the red line and then rotated clockwise. The sanwa button is shaken left and right and pressed down.)

![Step 12](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/12.01.png)

The corresponding position of the button on the PCB — New PCB:

![New PCB](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/新主板-1.png)

Old PCB:

![Old PCB](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/旧主板-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the button panel back on the controller">

Put the button panel back on the controller.

![Step 13](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/13-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Replace the turntable panel — pry off the four corner pads">

Replace the turntable panel, pry off the four corner pads.

![Step 14](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/14-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Pry off the four corner pads">

Pry off the four corner pads.

![Step 15](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/15-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws securing the housing">

Remove the screws securing the housing.

![Step 16](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/16-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Peel off the turntable sticker">

Peel off the turntable sticker.

![Step 17](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/17-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws securing the turntable">

Remove the screws securing the turntable.

![Step 18](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/18-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Lift the turntable out">

Press down on one side of the turntable with one hand to lift the other side, while the other hand lifts the turntable out of the raised part.

![Step 19](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/19-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Take out the felt and washer">

Take out the felt and washer.

![Step 20](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/20-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screw fixing the aluminum plate">

Use a 2.5mm hexagon wrench to remove the screw fixing the aluminum plate. Please pay attention to the nut with the fixing screw inside. When removing, please hold down the nut to prevent loss.

![Step 21](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/21-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Use a hex wrench">

Use a hex wrench.

![Step 22](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/22-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Hold down the fixed nut">

Hold down the fixed nut.

![Step 23](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/23-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the turntable part in its entirety">

The turntable part can be removed in its entirety after removing the bolts and nuts.

![Step 24](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/24-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the turntable part">

Remove the turntable part.

![Step 25](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/25-3.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Take out the panel">

Take out the panel.

![Step 26](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/26-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Arrange the new panels in order">

Arrange the new panels in order. (If you like the black background, please remove ② the silver cardboard, and note that the black hole is on the upper right.)

![Step 27](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/27-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Neatly arranged">

Neatly arranged.

![Step 28](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/28-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the light guide ring and aluminum plate back in place">

Put the light guide ring and aluminum plate back in place.

![Step 29](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/29-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Tighten the bolts and nuts that fix the aluminum plate">

Tighten the bolts and nuts that fix the aluminum plate. (After inserting the nut into the fixed position, hold down the nut and tighten the screw.)

![Step 30](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/30-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the felt and washer back in place">

Put the felt and washer back in place, being careful not to overlap.

![Step 31](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/31.01.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the turntable back in place and tighten the screws">

Put the turntable back in place and tighten the screws.

![Step 32](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/31.02-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Fit the upper and lower shells together">

First remove the button panel, align the ① and confirm that the wire at ② will not be stuck, and then fit the upper and lower shells together.

![Step 33](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/32-2.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Replace the retaining screws and attach the pads">

Replace the retaining screws on the back and attach the pads to the four corners.

![Step 34a](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/33.01.png)

![Step 34b](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-new-panel/33.02.png)

</Step>
</Steps>
