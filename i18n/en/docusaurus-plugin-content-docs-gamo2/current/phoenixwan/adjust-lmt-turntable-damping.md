# How to adjust the PHOENIXWAN LMT turntable damping

![Overview](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-00-898x1024.png)

<Steps>
<Step title="Remove the magnetic turntable sticker">

Remove the magnetic turntable sticker.

![Step 1](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-01.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the screws holding the turntable in place">

Use a Phillips screwdriver to remove the screws holding the turntable in place.

![Step 2](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-02.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the turntable">

Remove the turntable.

![Step 3](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-03.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Adjust the number of washers">

Adjust the number of washers. (Black×1=0.05mm, transparent×1=0.5mm)

![Step 4a](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-04.png)

![Step 4b](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-041.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Place the turntable over the spacer and align the screw holes">

Place the turntable over the spacer and align the screw holes.

![Step 5](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-05.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Tighten the screws to secure the turntable">

Use a screwdriver to tighten the screws to secure the turntable.

![Step 6](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-06.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Cover with magnetic turntable sticker">

Cover with magnetic turntable sticker.

![Step 7](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/adjust-lmt-turntable-damping/P1-LMT-damping-07.png)

</Step>
</Steps>
