# Replace the PHOENIXWAN Audio Light Metal Mesh

## Tools:

Phillips screwdriver.

![Tools](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/01.png)

<Steps>
<Step title="Pry off the four corner pads">

Pry off the four corner pads.

![Step 1](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/14.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Use a knife to lift off the foot pads">

Use a knife or other tool to lift off the foot pads.

![Step 2](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/15.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove all the screws securing the frame">

Remove all the screws securing the frame.

![Step 3](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/16.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Open the back cover and unplug the audio light cable">

Open the back cover and unplug the audio light cable.

![Step 4](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/04.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Take the fixed black cables out of the clip">

Take the fixed black cables out of the clip.

![Step 5](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/05.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Unscrew the 2 screws on the audio light">

Use a screwdriver to unscrew the 2 screws on the audio light.

![Step 6](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/06.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the audio light">

Remove the audio light.

![Step 7](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/07.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Remove the audio light mesh and light guide">

Fold back the bent part of the audio light mesh and remove the audio light mesh and light guide.

![Step 8a](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/13.png)

![Step 8b](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/12.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Replace the audio light mesh with a new one">

Replace the audio light mesh with a new one, and bend the protruding part to fix the audio light mesh.

![Step 9](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/14-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Put the audio light back on and fix it with screws">

Put the audio light back on and fix it with screws.

![Step 10](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/06-1.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Plug the audio light plug back in">

Plug the audio light plug back in and secure the wire harness in the white clip.

![Step 11](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/11.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Fit the upper and lower shells together">

First remove the button panel, align the ① and confirm that the wire at ② will not be stuck, and then fit the upper and lower shells together.

![Step 12](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/32.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Reinstall all the screws securing the frame">

Reinstall all the screws securing the frame.

![Step 13](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/33.01.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Attach the pads to the four corners">

Attach the pads to the four corners.

![Step 14](/img/docs/gamo2/phoenixwan/replace-audio-light-metal-mesh/33.02.png)

</Step>
</Steps>
