## 3. Firmware Flash Tutorial

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter Flash Mode">

**Press and hold** the BE2PS button and insert it into the USB port of the computer. After **1 second**, release the button.

</Step>
<Step title="Connect Software">

Unzip the firmware archive. Open `Update V1.1.exe` and click **Connect**.

If **Connect turns green**, the connection is successful. Otherwise, repeat step 1.

</Step>
<Step title="Select Firmware File">

Click the **Open files** button and select the firmware file `xxxxx.bin` to be upgraded.

</Step>
<Step title="Start Upgrade">

Click the **Update** button.

</Step>
<Step title="Wait for Completion">

Wait for the progress bar to finish. The upgrade is complete.

</Step>
</Steps>
