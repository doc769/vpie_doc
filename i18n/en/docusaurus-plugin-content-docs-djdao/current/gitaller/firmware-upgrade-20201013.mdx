## Firmware upgrade tutorial

<Steps>
<Step title="Uncompress the firmware archive">

Uncompress the downloaded firmware archive to a local folder.

</Step>
<Step title="Confirm USB is unplugged">

Confirm your GITALLER USB cable is unplugged from PC.

</Step>
<Step title="Enter firmware upgrade mode">

Push START + SELECT buttons at the same time, do not release. Plug GITALLER USB cable to PC with your other hand. Then release the START + SELECT buttons.

![Push START + SELECT](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step3-buttons.png)

![Plug in USB](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step3-usb.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Open firmware upgrade program">

Open `GITALLER_firmware_APP.exe`.

![Open program](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step4-app.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Load firmware file">

Click the **LoadFile** button, find your downloaded firmware folder, select `GITALLER_firmware_V1.1_20201013_Sensitivity_14.bin`.

The suffix Sensitivity_12 means lower sensitivity, Sensitivity_15 means higher sensitivity. We recommend Sensitivity_14 firmware.

![Click LoadFile](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step5-loadfile.png)

![Select firmware file](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step6-select-bin.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Start upgrade">

Click the **Start** button to begin updating. Wait for the progress bar to complete.

![Click Start](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step7-start.png)

![Wait for progress bar](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step8-progress.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Upgrade complete">

After success, **replug your GITALLER USB cable** (very important).

![Upgrade successful](/img/docs/gitaller/firmware-20201013/step9-success.png)

:::warning
If not successful, please retry from step 3.
:::

</Step>
</Steps>

In GITADORA trial, the first button shows as 0, wailing_up shows as 10, wailing_down shows as 11.
