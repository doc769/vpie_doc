# TAIKOLLER Disassembly Guide

<Steps>

<Step title="Remove the drumhead">

Please follow the technique shown in the diagram, take the whole drumhead out of the buckle and then remove it as a whole.

![Remove the drumhead](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step1.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove the drum stand">

Place the drum face down and remove the drum stand by rotating the hand screw counterclockwise.

![Remove the drum stand](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove the back screws">

Open the control box door, use the supplied screwdriver to remove all the screws on the back.

![Remove the back screws](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step3.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Open the housing">

Take away the shell and place it on the side, then open the tie that holds the cushion foam of the connector.

![Open the housing 1](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step4-1.png)

![Open the housing 2](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step4-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Unplug the sensor connectors">

Unplug the connectors of the 4 sets of sensors.

![Unplug sensor connectors 1](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step5-1.png)

![Unplug sensor connectors 2](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step5-2.png)

![Unplug sensor connectors 3](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step5-3.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove the cable ties">

Disassemble the 3 wire ties, remove the wires from the ties and place them neatly.

![Remove the cable ties](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step6.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove the back nuts">

Use an M6 (H10) wrench to remove the nuts on the back.

:::danger Important
Place the nuts on a safe area of the table according to their layout orientation to ensure they will not be confused. When reinstalling, the nuts must be installed in numerical order!!
:::

![Remove back nuts 1](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step7-1.png)

![Remove back nuts 2](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step7-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove the panels">

Insert the connector of the panel into the corresponding hole, then take out the corresponding panel. Be careful not to forcefully pull the sensor wire.

:::warning Caution
- It is recommended not to remove more than one panel at a time to avoid installation difficulties.
- When removing the panel, be careful not to touch the nuts to ensure their sorting position will not be confused!!
:::

![Remove panels 1](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step8-1.png)

![Remove panels 2](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step8-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove the sensors">

Use a screwdriver to unscrew the corresponding screws and remove the parts holding the sensor in place, then remove the sensor positioning screws.

:::warning Caution
Be careful not to confuse the nut sorting position!!
:::

![Remove sensors 1](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step9-1.png)

![Remove sensors 2](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step9-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Install the nuts">

Take care to tighten the nuts in place when installing them.

![Install the nuts](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step10.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Install the housing">

When installing the housing, pull out the control box wire first to avoid the wire being stuck inside the housing.

![Install the housing 1](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step11-1.png)

![Install the housing 2](/img/docs/taikoller/disassembly/step11-2.png)

</Step>

</Steps>
