# How to Install ONTROLLER

<Steps>

<Step title="Unboxing">

Unboxing, the picture shows the front.

![Unboxing](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step1.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove Cushioning Material">

Remove the cushioning material in the order shown.

![Remove cushioning](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Prepare Installation Tools">

Prepare installation tools.

![Tools 1](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step3-1.png)

![Tools 2](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step3-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove Side Button Panels">

Use tool C (2.5mm) to remove the outer decorative panels of the L and R side buttons.

![Remove panels](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step4.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Unscrew and Remove Panels">

Turn the hex wrench in the direction shown, unscrew the screws (keep them in the tool box), and remove the panels.

![Unscrew panels](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step5.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Remove Protective Film">

Tear off the protective film of the L and R panels and place the panels in a safe place.

![Remove protective film](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step6-film.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Take Out the Main Unit">

Take out the main unit, place it in a convenient place for operation, and place the L and R side buttons on the corresponding sides.

![Take out main unit](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step6.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Assemble R Side Button">

Assemble the R side button.

![Assemble R side](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step7.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Locate Right Connector">

From the side, you can see that the right plug is pasted with paper tape on the side of the main body.

![Locate connector](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step8.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Connect Right Cable">

Tear off the tape, gently pull the connector (remember not to pull with force), and insert the plug (the plug has a correct orientation — do not force it in).

![Pull connector](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step9-1.png)

![Insert connector](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step9-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Connection">

The wire is plugged in as shown in the picture.

![Connected state](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step10.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Secure the Cable">

Secure the wire in the clamp as shown.

![Secure cable](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step11.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Close R Side Button">

Assemble the R side button.

:::danger Important
During the closing process, do not press the wire to the iron position. If you find it difficult to install smoothly, check the wire routing.
:::

![Close R side](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step12.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Fix R Side Outer Screw">

Use tool D (4mm) to fix the screw on the R side.

:::warning
There is a risk of tipping if the screws are not fastened!
:::

![Fix R screw](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step13.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Pre-tighten 5 Silver A Screws">

First, pre-tighten the 5 silver A screws. Do not tighten them completely to ensure that the side buttons can still move.

![Pre-tighten A screws 1](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step14-1.png)

![Pre-tighten A screws 2](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step14-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Pre-tighten 2 Black B Screws">

Then, pre-tighten the 2 black B screws. Do not tighten them completely to ensure that the side buttons can still move.

![Pre-tighten B screws](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step15.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Fully Tighten 7 Screws">

Then, completely tighten the 7 screws as shown in the picture.

![Tighten screws](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step16.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Attach R Panel (Pre-tighten)">

Cover the R side panel, use tool C (2.5mm) to fix the screws, first pre-tighten 4 screws, do not tighten them completely.

![Attach R panel pre-tighten](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step17.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Fully Tighten R Panel Screws">

Then, tighten the 4 screws as shown.

![Tighten R panel screws](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step18.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Assemble L Side Button">

Assemble the L side button.

![Assemble L side](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step19.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Locate Left Connector">

From the side, you can see that the left plug is pasted with paper tape on the side of the main body.

![Locate left connector](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step20.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Connect Left Cable">

Tear off the tape, gently pull the connector (remember not to pull with force), and insert the plug (the plug has a correct orientation — do not force it in).

![Pull left connector](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step21-1.png)

![Insert left connector](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step21-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Left Connection">

The wire is plugged in as shown in the picture.

![Left connected state](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step22.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Secure the Left Cable">

Secure the wire in the clamp as shown.

![Secure left cable](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step23.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Close L Side Button">

Assemble the L side button.

:::danger Important
During the closing process, do not press the wire to the iron position. If you find it difficult to install smoothly, check the wire routing.
:::

![Close L side](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step24.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Fix L Side Outer Screw">

Use tool D (4mm) to fix the screw on the L side.

:::warning
There is a risk of tipping if the screws are not fastened!
:::

![Fix L screw](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step25.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Pre-tighten Left 5 A Screws">

First, pre-tighten the 5 silver A screws. Do not tighten them completely to ensure that the side buttons can still move.

![Pre-tighten left A screws 1](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step26-1.png)

![Pre-tighten left A screws 2](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step26-2.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Pre-tighten Left 2 B Screws">

Then, pre-tighten the 2 black B screws. Do not tighten them completely to ensure that the side buttons can still move.

![Pre-tighten left B screws](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step27.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Fully Tighten Left 7 Screws">

Then, completely tighten the 7 screws as shown in the picture.

![Tighten left screws](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step28.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Attach L Panel (Pre-tighten)">

Cover the L side panel, use tool C (2.5mm) to fix the screws, first pre-tighten 4 screws, do not tighten them completely.

![Attach L panel pre-tighten](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step29.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Fully Tighten L Panel Screws">

Then, tighten the 4 screws as shown.

![Tighten L panel screws](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step30.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Assembly Complete">

The assembly is complete and the finished product is as shown in the picture.

![Assembly complete](/img/docs/ontroller/installation/step31.png)

</Step>

</Steps>
